MISSION INTEL

01. AUDIO TRIGGER

The system is synced to your microphone. To fire a heavy kinetic slug, make a sharp, loud sound like a clap or a knock on your desk.

02. TARGETING

Your blaster moves vertically automatically. Time your sound so the projectile intersects with the neon containers rising from the bottom.

03. SYSTEM COOLDOWN

One sound equals one fire. The blaster has a 400ms cooldown to prevent accidental double-firing. Focus on precision, not noise.
